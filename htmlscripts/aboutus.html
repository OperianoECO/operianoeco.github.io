Our Story

Hey there friend! I'm Yuriel Operiano. I'm currently a Year 1 studying Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics at Nanyang Polytechnic!

My hobbies include:
  • Badminton
  • Playing Valorant
  • Coding in Python and Javascript
  • Watching Anime

I'm also the founder of FriendsMC. This is actually my project for Semester 1 Web Development, and I decided to base it on this server that I've founded with my friends. Feel free to look around and explore this website. Have fun!

On the 8th of May 2023, Yuriel Operiano was chatting with his secondary school friends on WhatsApp about life, reminsicing about their secondary school days and cherishing the fun they had together. Suddenly, the topic of Minecraft was brought up; the group had made one amazing Minecraft server back in Secondary 2 where many great experiences and relationships were forged between everyone.

Suddenly, Yuriel had concocted an idea to make another Minecraft server where they could all play again! He envisioned the server to be one with the goal of having as much fun as possible with friends. He proposed the idea of building grand structures, battling fearsome creatures, and embarking on thrilling adventures with the new release of Minecraft 1.20 and the Deep Dark. Yuriel's friends shared the same sentiment as him; excitement filled the air as they began to eagerly discuss their vision for the server. They wanted it to be a place where players, even if not within their own class, could come together, share their creativity, and forge lasting friendships.

Thus, the idea of FriendsMC was born. Yuriel and friends wasted no time in putting their plan into action: Yuriel set up another Microsoft account to gain a free trial to host a Minecraft Realms server while the rest of the friends pooled their time and resources to establish a server dynamic and invite new, enterprising people into the realm.

Today, FriendsMC continues to unfold as a shining server of the power of friendship, creativity, and the boundless possibilities Minecraft offers. As the server continues to grow with much more people entering the fray, grand structures were erected, as well as numerous ambitious projects that its players are pursuing as we speak.

This is only the beginning, will you create your own story in FriendsMC?
